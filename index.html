Frequently Asked Questions

Simplify your business finances with Trubilling — your all-in-one invoicing and expense management platform. Create and send invoices in seconds, automate expense tracking, and manage everything from a clean, user-friendly dashboard. Stay organized, reduce errors, and keep your business running smoothly.

How do I create and send an invoice on Trubilling?

Creating an invoice is simple — just fill in your client details, add your services or products, and click “Send”. Trubilling automatically generates a professional, branded invoice and delivers it directly to your client’s email.

Can I track expenses and categorize them automatically?

Yes! Trubilling allows you to upload receipts, enter expenses manually, and categorize them instantly. The system also supports automated tracking, helping you stay tax-ready without stress.

Does Trubilling support multi-user access for my team?

Absolutely. Depending on your plan, you can add team members, assign permissions, and allow them to manage invoices, expenses, or reports. This makes collaboration seamless for growing businesses.
